The flagship event of Oasis, BITS-Pilani, one of the oldest cultural festivals in the country. About RapWars

Seven years ago, a tiny idea creeped into the minds of the organisers of OASIS. Today, that idea has blossomed into one of the largest pan-India rap battle competitions. Rap Wars, the rap battle event of OASIS, BITS Pilani adds bars and pouches to this age old cultural fest of India. In the seven years to this date, we have risen, tumbled, had fun, fallen, enraptured, scratched ourselves, enthralled, been abused and entertained. We kept going. Esteemed artists like Raftaar, Vivian Divine, Rapture, Poetik Justis, Cizzy, Quixotic and many more have kept the audience on their feet at various editions of this event. This is your chance to make a mark.
To shake the floor and pop up the battle auditions are going to be held in Delhi, Mumbai and Kolkata to get the best battlers onto the floor.
Winners of the elimination rounds get an all-expense trip to Pilani during OASIS, 2018 to battle it out with the winners from the other cities; cash prizes worth over 30k and a chance to rise phenomenally in the public eye amongst other rewards.
About Purple Prose

BITS, Pilani brings to you, 'Purple Prose : The Poetry Slam', a one of a kind platform for poets across the country to give their emotions, a thought and their thoughts, a word. With a chance to entertain student community of India's one of the largest institute, Purple Prose promises to take creative potential of an individual to national scale. So, breathe in experience and breathe out poetry.
About Standup Soapbox

Oasis, BITS PILANI, brings to you the second edition of Stand-up Soapbox, one of the largest stand-up competitions for amateur comedians out there. Stand-up soapbox is one of the flagship events of our cultural festival. This event is geared towards finding new talents in the stand-up world and giving them an opportunity to share a stage with the celebrities of the industry.
Eliminations will be held in bengaluru, delhi and mumbai. The winners of this round will get an all expenses paid trip to Pilani and an amazing opportunity to open for established comedians in front of a lively audience of around 2000 people. So, are you funny innuf?
